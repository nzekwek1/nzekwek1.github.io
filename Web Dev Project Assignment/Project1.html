About Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa. It is the most populous country in Africa comprising of 36 states and a nations capital called Abuja. The three largest ethnic groups are the Hausa-Fulani in the north, Yoruba in the west and the Igbo in the east. Each ethnic group is associated with a specific religion and being a part of the Igbo ethnic group, I was raised Christian like evryone else who identify as Igbo.
